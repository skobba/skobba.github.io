# Exchanges

## NASDAQ and NYSE Opening Hours
* CET - Central European Time
* CEST - Central European Summer Time


### CET
* Regular Trading Hours: 15:30 to 22:00
* Pre-market Hours: 10:00 to 15:30
* After-hours Trading: 22:00 to 02:00

### CEST
* Regular Trading Hours: 15:30 to 22:00
* Pre-market Hours: 10:00 to 15:30
* After-hours Trading: 22:00 to 02:00
